ChopLight Privacy Policy

Effective date: March 15, 2026

Overview

ChopLight is a flashlight utility app that lets you toggle your phone's flashlight with a double-chop gesture. We respect your privacy and are committed to transparency about how the app works.

Data Collection

ChopLight does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal data. The app operates entirely on your device with no network communication other than Google Play Billing for in-app purchases.

Permissions

  • Camera: Used solely to control the camera flash (flashlight). No photos or videos are captured.
  • Vibration: Used to provide haptic feedback when the flashlight toggles (Pro feature).
  • Foreground Service: Keeps the chop gesture detection running in the background so the flashlight can be toggled from the lock screen.
  • Boot Completed: Restarts the service automatically after a device reboot if you had it enabled.
  • Billing: Used to process in-app purchases through Google Play for the Pro upgrade.

Sensor Data

The app reads your device's accelerometer to detect the chop gesture. This sensor data is processed entirely on-device in real time and is never recorded, stored, or transmitted.

In-App Purchases

ChopLight offers an optional Pro upgrade through Google Play Billing. All payment processing is handled by Google. We do not have access to your payment information.

Third-Party Services

The app does not include any analytics, advertising, or tracking SDKs. The only third-party service used is Google Play Billing for in-app purchases.

Children's Privacy

ChopLight does not collect any data from anyone, including children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

If this policy is updated, the revised version will be posted at this URL with an updated effective date.

Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, please open an issue on our app's support page on Google Play.
